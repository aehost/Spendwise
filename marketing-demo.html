How It Works

Set up in
under 30 seconds.

No bank login. No data sharing. No complicated setup.

1

Download & Sign Up

Create a free account with your email. That's the only information SpendWise ever needs from you.

2

Allow SMS Permission

Grant read-SMS access. SpendWise only reads bank messages — never personal conversations. All processing happens on your phone.

3

Add Your Bank Accounts

Enter your bank name and last 4 digits of your account number. SpendWise auto-matches incoming SMS to the right account from that point on.

4

Set Salary & Budgets

Tell SpendWise your monthly salary and set budget limits per category. Now you have a complete financial picture — automatically maintained.

5

Live Your Life — We Track It

Every bank SMS gets captured, parsed, and categorised in real time. Bank balance stays current. Budgets update live. Reports build themselves.
