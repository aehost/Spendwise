# SpendWise — Video Scripts & Production Guide

---

## VIDEO 1: Marketing / Registration Ad (60–90 seconds)

### Production Notes
- Tone: Professional, warm, aspirational — like a Zerodha or PhonePe ad
- Pacing: Fast cuts, 2–4 seconds per scene
- Music: Uplifting lo-fi or soft electronic — e.g. "Inspiring" by Scott Buckley (free), or search "corporate inspiring" on Pixabay Music
- Voiceover: Male or female, confident, Indian English accent
- On-screen text: Large, bold, white on dark background — fade in with each scene
- Resolution: 1080×1920 (vertical / Reels) or 1920×1080 (YouTube)

---

### SCENE-BY-SCENE STORYBOARD

**[0:00 – 0:06] HOOK — THE PAIN**
- Visual: Split screen — left: person staring at confusing bank statement; right: mounting UPI notifications
- On-screen text: "Where did my salary go?"
- VO: *"You check your balance. It's lower than you expected. You have no idea why."*

**[0:06 – 0:12] RELATABILITY**
- Visual: Phone screen showing dozens of unread bank SMS notifications
- On-screen text: "You have 47 unread bank messages"
- VO: *"Every day, your bank sends you the answer — but who has time to read 47 messages?"*

**[0:12 – 0:18] THE SOLUTION — APP REVEAL**
- Visual: Dark screen, SpendWise logo animates in with a soft glow
- On-screen text: "Introducing SpendWise"
- VO: *"SpendWise reads your bank messages — so you don't have to."*
- Sound effect: Soft chime / notification ping

**[0:18 – 0:26] FEATURE 1 — AUTO SMS IMPORT**
- Visual: Phone screen recording of a bank SMS arriving → SpendWise notification pops up → transaction appears in the app automatically
- On-screen text: "Instant SMS detection" → "Transaction logged automatically"
- VO: *"The moment money moves — SpendWise captures it. Amount, merchant, account number. Automatically."*

**[0:26 – 0:33] FEATURE 2 — MULTI-BANK**
- Visual: Home screen showing 3 coloured account cards with balances expanding
- On-screen text: "HDFC • SBI • Axis — one dashboard"
- VO: *"Multiple bank accounts? One clean view. Always consolidated, always current."*

**[0:33 – 0:40] FEATURE 3 — BUDGETS & INSIGHTS**
- Visual: Budget progress bars filling up across Food, Fuel, Shopping categories
- On-screen text: "Know before you overspend"
- VO: *"Set monthly budgets. SpendWise shows you exactly where you stand — in real time."*

**[0:40 – 0:47] FEATURE 4 — REPORTS**
- Visual: Monthly report page scrolling, showing savings rate, category pie chart
- On-screen text: "Professional monthly reports — downloaded in one tap"
- VO: *"Monthly reports built automatically. Share with your CA. Or just feel proud of your savings rate."*

**[0:47 – 0:54] SECURITY**
- Visual: Fingerprint icon appearing, then a lock icon — clean dark animation
- On-screen text: "Fingerprint locked • No bank login needed • 100% private"
- VO: *"Your data never leaves your phone. No bank passwords, no data selling. Just you — and your finances."*

**[0:54 – 1:00] PRICE / VALUE**
- Visual: ₹0.00 displayed large on screen with a tick mark
- On-screen text: "Free. Forever. No subscriptions."
- VO: *"And it's completely free. No subscriptions, no ads, no catch."*

**[1:00 – 1:10] CALL TO ACTION**
- Visual: Download button animation, app icon, phone mockup
- On-screen text: "Download SpendWise" → "Take control of your money today"
- VO: *"Download SpendWise. Set up in 30 seconds. And finally — know where your money goes."*
- End card: SpendWise logo + tagline "Smart money. Zero effort."

### CANVA PRODUCTION STEPS (Video 1)
1. Open Canva → New Design → Instagram Reel (1080×1920) or YouTube (1920×1080)
2. Use a dark template (search "dark finance")
3. Paste each scene's ON-SCREEN TEXT as a text layer
4. Add background: solid #07070F (near-black) for all slides
5. Accent color: #6C63FF (purple) for highlights
6. Music: Upload from Pixabay → set to auto-duck under voiceover
7. Record voiceover in Murf.ai or ElevenLabs → download MP3 → import into Canva
8. Export as MP4

## VIDEO 2: Feature Walkthrough (2:30 – 3:00 minutes)

### Production Notes
- Tone: Tutorial, clear and calm — like an app demo on YouTube
- Pacing: Slower, screen-led — let each screen breathe 4–6 seconds
- Music: Calm ambient background, very low volume (30%) — "Focus" playlists on YouTube Audio Library
- Voiceover: Friendly, instructive — explain every tap
- Visuals: ACTUAL app screen recordings from your Android device + animated page headers

---

### SCENE-BY-SCENE STORYBOARD

**[0:00 – 0:08] INTRO**
- Visual: SpendWise logo + dark background + animated tagline
- On-screen text: "SpendWise — Complete App Walkthrough"
- VO: *"In this video, I'll show you every screen in SpendWise and how to get the most out of each one."*

---

**[0:08 – 0:25] SCREEN 1: Home / Dashboard**
- Visual: Home screen recording — show salary bar, bank balance, recent transactions, bills
- On-screen labels (overlay arrows): Point to Salary Remaining, Bank Balance card, Recent Transactions, Bills section
- VO: *"This is your Home screen — your financial command centre. At a glance: how much of your salary is left, your current bank balance across all accounts, and the 5 most recent transactions."*
- Highlight: Tap bank balance → per-account breakdown expands
- VO: *"Tap the bank balance card to see a per-account breakdown — each account colour-coded with its last 4 digits."*

---

**[0:25 – 0:50] SCREEN 2: Transactions / Spends**
- Visual: Transactions page — scroll through list, show filter chips
- VO: *"The Transactions screen is the heart of SpendWise. Every debit and credit — whether from SMS or manually added — lives here."*
- Demo: Tap a bank chip filter → shows only that account's transactions
- VO: *"Filter by bank account — tap HDFC to see only HDFC transactions. Or filter by category: food, fuel, bills, entertainment."*
- Demo: Show a NEEDS TAG transaction → tap 🏷️ Tag → pick category → save
- VO: *"When a bank SMS arrives, it's automatically saved as pending. Tap Tag — pick the correct category, add a note, mark as wasteful if needed — and it's done."*
- Demo: Tap the SMS import button → show bank messages list
- VO: *"You can also tap the SMS button to manually browse your last 90 days of bank messages and import any transaction you missed."*

---

**[0:50 – 1:10] SCREEN 3: Budget Tracker**
- Visual: Budget page — show category progress bars
- VO: *"The Budget screen shows your monthly spend against your personal limits, category by category."*
- Highlight: Progress bar near limit turns amber → over limit shows red
- VO: *"Bars turn amber when you're close to the limit, red when you've exceeded it. It's your early warning system — so you never get a surprise at month end."*
- Demo: Tap to edit a budget → change Food from 5000 to 8000 → save
- VO: *"Budgets are fully customisable. Adjust any category to match your lifestyle."*

---

**[1:10 – 1:30] SCREEN 4: Money / Income Tracker**
- Visual: Money screen — salary card, salary history, bank balance
- VO: *"The Money screen is where you manage income. Set your monthly salary — SpendWise uses it to calculate savings rate and budget headroom."*
- Demo: Tap Mark Salary → enter amount → confirm
- VO: *"On salary day, tap Mark Salary. SpendWise records the income and automatically updates your bank balance."*
- Show salary history list
- VO: *"Your complete salary history is maintained automatically — useful for year-end planning or tax filing."*

---

**[1:30 – 1:50] SCREEN 5: Credit Cards & Loans**
- Visual: Credit cards page — show card with outstanding balance
- VO: *"If you have credit cards or loans — EMIs, home loan, personal loan — track them here."*
- Demo: Add a credit card → name, limit, outstanding → save
- VO: *"Add your card, set the limit and outstanding balance. SpendWise deducts EMI amounts automatically from your bank balance when the SMS arrives."*
- Show loan tracker
- VO: *"The Loan tracker shows total outstanding, monthly EMI, and links each bank transaction to the right loan automatically."*

---

**[1:50 – 2:05] SCREEN 6: Investments**
- Visual: Investments page — add investment → show monthly + total value
- VO: *"Track all your investments in one place — Zerodha, Groww, PPF, NPS, Fixed Deposits — whatever you have."*
- Demo: Add Mutual Fund SIP → 5000/month, current value 62000
- VO: *"Add the name, monthly amount, and current value. SpendWise tracks total invested, current value, and gives you a quick returns view."*

---

**[2:05 – 2:20] SCREEN 7: Settings**
- Visual: Settings page — scroll through sections
- VO: *"In Settings, you manage your bank accounts, bills, budgets, profile, and sync preferences."*
- Highlight: Bank Accounts section
- VO: *"The Bank Accounts section is where you add each of your bank accounts with their last 4 digits. Once added, every SMS is automatically matched to the right account."*
- Show cloud sync section
- VO: *"SpendWise syncs to Firebase cloud automatically. If you switch phones or reinstall, just log in — all your data comes back instantly."*

---

**[2:20 – 2:35] SCREEN 8: Reports**
- Visual: Monthly report page — select month → report renders → download button
- VO: *"The Monthly Report gives you a complete income vs. expense breakdown for any month. Savings rate, wasteful spending, category totals — everything in one professional report."*
- Demo: Tap download → share sheet appears
- VO: *"Download it as HTML, share it on WhatsApp, or email it to yourself — all in one tap."*

---

**[2:35 – 2:50] SECURITY & PRIVACY RECAP**
- Visual: Settings → biometric prompt appears
- VO: *"Every time SpendWise opens, biometric authentication is required. No fingerprint, no access. And screenshots are blocked by default — your financial data is for your eyes only."*

---

**[2:50 – 3:00] OUTRO**
- Visual: Home screen → SpendWise logo fade in
- On-screen text: "Download SpendWise Free • Works with all Indian banks • 30-second setup"
- VO: *"That's SpendWise — complete financial tracking, zero effort. Download it free and set up in 30 seconds."*

## RECOMMENDED FREE TOOLS

### Voiceover (AI)
- **ElevenLabs** (elevenlabs.io) — Best quality, 10 min/month free
  - Voice: "Adam" or "Rachel" for Indian English
- **Murf.ai** — Good alternative, free tier available

### Music (Royalty-Free, Safe for YouTube)
- **Pixabay Music** (pixabay.com/music) — Search "corporate inspiring"
- **YouTube Audio Library** — "Inspire" section
- **Recommended tracks**: "Inspiring" by Scott Buckley, "Better Days" genre

### Video Editing
- **Canva** — Easiest, drag-and-drop, has screen recording import
- **CapCut** — Great for mobile editing, transitions, text animations
- **DaVinci Resolve** — Professional, free desktop app

### Screen Recording (App footage)
- On your Android: Settings → Developer Options → Screen Recorder
- Or: Use scrcpy (free, mirrors Android to PC, then record with OBS)
